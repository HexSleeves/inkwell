# What Is a Digital Garden?

A digital garden is a space for **growing ideas in public**. Notes are not
polished, dated blog posts — they are living documents that you revisit, link
together, and refine.

The defining feature is the connections between notes. In this garden those
connections are [[wikilinks-and-backlinks]]: write `[[another-note]]` and the
two notes become part of one graph.

If you are new here, the [[welcome]] note is the best place to start. When you
are ready to let an agent tend the garden with you, see [[reading-with-ai]].
